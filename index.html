About
The Workshop

The goal is to introduce you to a variety of the features available and the flexibility of the Shopify platform so you can better communicate to your clients what can be accomplished with Shopify. This could also be a great opportunity to see if Shopify could be a good fit for your business.

We will introduce platform features, like dynamic sections and new APIs. We'll also highlight some unique use cases for Shopify.

The Workshop will be held Wednesday January 31st at WeWork Sony Center.

Register to attend

What
We'll Cover

  1. CMS organization and out of the box available resources (i.e. products, collections, blogs, pages etc.)
  2. Advanced functionality and customization with static and dynamic sections
  3. Advanced feature development with Shopify APIs (javascript SDK, StoreFront API, Admin API)
